
# Primal Perfomance Lab
**Primal Fitness Platform**
*2024*

- **Project Overview:** Primal is a premium fitness center equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. The core objective of this project was to build a comprehensive digital platform. Beyond introducing the brand, the system featured a personalized member portal where users could log in and track their training progress, with complex health data aggregated seamlessly from external IoT devices (Myzone & Conner).

- **My Role:** I was brought on board as the core Project Manager and Lead UI/UX Designer, trusted to shape both the brand's visual identity and the platform's digital experience from the ground up.

- **Key Contributions:** I successfully conceptualized and designed the overarching brand identity, including the Primal logo. For the platform, I conducted in-depth feature research, mapped out complete wireframes, and delivered high-fidelity UI/UX designs for the entire system. Furthermore, I directed and managed both the frontend and backend engineering teams, ensuring the final product perfectly aligned with the design vision and effectively handled the external data integration.

# Triip.me
**Peer-to-Peer Travel Platform**
*2019*

- **Project Overview:** Triip.me is an innovative, global peer-to-peer travel marketplace. The platform disrupts traditional tourism by empowering locals and travel enthusiasts to create, publish, and sell their own unique, authentic tour experiences directly to travelers worldwide. Validating its strong product vision and market potential, the project successfully secured investment on the hit TV show **Shark Tank**.

- **My Role:** I joined this dynamic startup ecosystem as a UI/UX Designer, tasked with shaping the digital experience of a high-potential platform.

- **Key Contributions:** I was responsible for designing an intuitive, trust-building user interface for a complex two-sided marketplace. My work focused on crafting seamless, user-friendly flows for both sides of the ecosystem: enabling "hosts" to easily package and monetize their local tours, while providing travelers with a smooth, engaging discovery and booking experience. The polished and highly functional design played a part in making the product investor-ready.

# Ants.vn
**Multi-Channel Advertising Platform (AdTech)**
*2017*

- **Project Overview:** Ants.vn is a comprehensive, multi-channel advertising booking and management system, operating as a robust Ad Network akin to Google Ads and Admicro. The platform empowers advertisers to set up complex ad campaigns, manage budgets, and track real-time performance analytics across diverse digital channels.

- **My Role:** I was brought on board to lead the "Zero to One" creative and product design phase, responsible for building the brand's visual identity and architecting the entire platform's user experience.

- **Key Contributions:** I successfully conceptualized and designed the overarching brand identity, starting with the core logo design. On the product side, I tackled the inherent complexity of an AdTech B2B system by mapping out comprehensive wireframes and delivering the final UI/UX design. My focus was on transforming heavy, complex data streams into clean, intuitive dashboards and streamlining the multi-step campaign creation flows, ensuring maximum efficiency and usability for advertisers.
